# Privacy Policy

### Welcome to Joshi Codes!

I take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information I collect, how I use it, and how I protect it when you visit my website, **joshi.codes**.

---

### 1. Information I Collect

The only personal information I collect is your **email address**, and that's only if you choose to subscribe to my email list.

I may also collect non-personal information about your visit, such as your IP address, and device type. This is done through standard technology like cookies.

---

### 2. How I Use Your Information

I will only use your email address to send you new articles and occasional newsletters. I will **never** share your email address with any third-party companies for marketing purposes.

---

### 3. Cookies and Tracking

I use cookies and similar technologies to understand how people use the website. This helps me improve the site's functionality and your user experience.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, but doing so might affect how some parts of the site work.

---

### 4. Your Choices

You can **unsubscribe** from my email list at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email I send you.

---

### 5. Data Retention

I will keep your email address for as long as you're subscribed. Once you unsubscribe, I will delete your email from my records.

---

### 6. Security

I take reasonable steps to protect your information, but please know that no website or internet transmission is 100% secure. I cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

---

### 7. Children's Privacy

This website is not intended for children under 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent and believe your child has given me their personal information, please contact me, and I will delete it immediately.

---

### 8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy at any time by posting a new version on this page. It's a good idea to check back here periodically to stay informed about any changes.

---

### 9. Contact Me

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact me at dirghayujoshi@icloud.com.
